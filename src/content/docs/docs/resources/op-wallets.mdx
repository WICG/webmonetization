*Last updated: October 22, 2024*

The following wallet providers support sending and receiving Web Monetization payments. 

<div style="border: 1px solid #357873; padding: 10px;">
<Icon name="information" color="#357873" size="2rem" class="inline-icon" /> Be sure to select a provider that is available in your **region** and supports your preferred **currency**.
</div>

Your provider will assign you a payment address, called either a wallet address or a payment pointer. Your payment address uniquely identifies your wallet account with your provider and can be shared publicly--similar to how your email address identifies your email account and can be shared publicly.
